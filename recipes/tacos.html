Tacos

Description

This is a recipe to make tacos in the most simple way i know.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Cut the coriander and onion in small pieces.
  3. Heat the tortillas.
  4. On the tortilla put some meat, then coriander and onion at will.
  5. Eat.
